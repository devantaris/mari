V2 — RESOLUTION

Second Opinion Resolution

A calibrated SVM provides an independent assessment of abstained cases. If P(fraud) < 0.01, the transaction is safely cleared to APPROVE at the cost-optimal breakeven point.

V3 — FUSION

Dempster-Shafer Evidence Fusion

Three independent evidence sources — ensemble probability, anomaly detector, and calibrated SVM — are combined using Dempster's rule of combination. Conflict and ignorance metrics enable formal sub-routing of escalated cases.

💡 Escalations decomposed into AUTO_DECLINE, STEP_UP, and HUMAN_ESCALATE using belief functions.

Terminal State Collapse + SHAP Explainability

All intermediate states collapse into 4 clean terminal actions. Every uncertain case receives a SHAP reason code identifying the top-3 features driving the decision — full explainability with zero human involvement.

Three Pillars of Decision

MARI evaluates every transaction along three independent epistemic axes before routing to the optimal action.

Calibrated Risk

Five isotonically calibrated XGBoost models trained on bootstrap resamples. The mean probability is the risk score; the standard deviation is the uncertainty.

Predictive Uncertainty

When models disagree (σ ≥ 0.02), the system recognizes its own epistemic limits and routes to cautious actions rather than making overconfident decisions.

Novelty Isolation

An Isolation Forest trained exclusively on legitimate behavior flags zero-day patterns the ensemble has never seen — a structural defense against distribution shift.
