The Problem

Binary classifiers output a probability. Above threshold: fraud. Below: legitimate. But what happens when the model is uncertain? When it encounters patterns it has never seen? When the cost of being wrong is asymmetric? Binary systems can't say "I don't know." MARI can.

The Progression
V1
Abstention

Bootstrap ensemble routes uncertain cases to abstention instead of forcing a decision.

5 states · σ ≥ 0.02 triggers caution
V2
Resolution

Fusion
